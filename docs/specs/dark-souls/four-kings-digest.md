# The Four Kings (Mega Boss) — Rulebook Digest

Source: `games/dark-souls/rulebooks/four-kings.pdf` (16 pp, official Steamforged rules insert).
Page refs are PDF pages (= printed pages).

## What it is (p2)

First-class **mega boss** expansion: a boss faced AFTER a mini boss and a main boss have been beaten. Adds the shared mega-boss framework (level 4 encounters, Mega Boss game board) plus the Four Kings themselves.

## Contents (p4)

- 1x Rules Insert
- 4x Four Kings Miniatures, 4x Health Dials, **1x shared Data Card**, 4x Identifier Tokens
- 20x Behaviour Cards, 2x Treasure Cards
- 4x Level 4 Encounter Cards, 1x Mega Boss Game Board (double-sided)

## Shared mega-boss framework (identical text in all three mega boss books)

### Setup after the main boss (p7)
- Add main-boss treasure to inventory; box all tiles except the Bonfire tile.
- Place the double-sided **Mega Boss game board** with its doorway aligned to a Bonfire doorway, encounter side up (enemy + terrain spawn nodes, NOT the mega-boss-spawn side).
- Reset sparks normally. Skip setup steps 5–7 (no changes to character boards / treasure deck / tokens).
- Instead of four encounters: exactly **one level 4 encounter**, drawn at random from the expansion's four L4 cards, placed face down on the board. If a revealed L4 card requires expansion components you don't own, randomly draw a different one.

### Level 4 encounter cards (p8)
- Same card anatomy as L1–3 (name, enemy spawn, terrain spawn, trap icons, difficulty, required sets, set symbol; core p17) but with **twice as many enemy and terrain spawn nodes**, many more trapped nodes on trapped versions, and **all four wall nodes beside the doorway are entry nodes** (vs three).
- Clearing it: remove all black and red cubes from endurance bars, add **8 souls per character** to the soul cache.
- Once defeated, an L4 encounter can never be replayed, and it does NOT reset when the party rests at the bonfire (pp8, 14).

### Setup after the L4 encounter (p9)
- Characters go to the Bonfire tile. May visit Blacksmith Andre and/or the Firekeeper; **no further exploration**; **no free rest** (resting costs a spark).
- Clear the board, flip it to the mega-boss-spawn side, align doorway with Bonfire doorway, place the Fog Gate token on that doorway.

### Mega boss basics (pp10-11)
- Follow standard enemy/boss rules except as noted. Mega boss data cards = boss data cards with a **mega boss icon**; fields: name, threat level, behaviour deck size, starting health, heat up point, block/resist, special ability, icon+set symbol (p11).
- Ending: defeating the mega boss wins the game; its treasure cards matter only in custom campaigns (core p32) (p12).

Four Kings L4 encounter names seen in this book: **New Londo Ruins** (pp5, 8), **Cursed Cavern** (p9).

## Four Kings data card (pp5, 11)

- Threat Level: **X** (none — see activation-order rule below)
- Starting Health: **25** (per king; each of the four kings has his own numbered health dial)
- Behaviour Deck Size: **4**
- Heat Up Point: **–** (never heats up in the standard way)
- Block **2** / Resist **2**
- Special Ability — **Royal Summons**: "The encounter starts with King One. The first three times you would flip over the behaviour deck, perform a summoning heat up instead."

## Starting the encounter (p12)

1. Characters on entry nodes (max 3 models/node); Aggro token on one character; **King One** model on the mega boss spawn node, front arc facing board centre.
2. Behaviour deck build: separate the **8 standard**, **4 second-king**, **4 third-king**, **4 fourth-king** cards. Take **4 random standard** cards, shuffle.
3. Reveal one random card of the four per gravestone found in the L4 encounter.
4. Shuffle; deck ready.

## Custom game elements (p13)

- **One through Four**: party must kill all four kings. Kings share ONE data card and ONE behaviour deck; during a boss activation, EVERY king in play performs the flipped card's behaviours. No threat level: multiple kings activate in numerical order (king one, then two, ...).
- Identifier tokens on model bases tie king model N to health dial N.
- **Take a Breather**: if zero boss models are in play at a boss activation (possible between kills and summons), still flip a behaviour card, then each character gains 1 Health, and the activation ends.
- **Royal Summons** procedure — the first three times the behaviour deck is empty at the start of the Four Kings' activation (instead of flipping the discard pile face down):
  1. Place the next king model (2, then 3, then 4) on the mega boss spawn node; models on that node are pushed to an adjacent node (resolved like the Leap icon).
  2. Randomly remove one card from the behaviour deck (unseen).
  3. Add **two random** behaviour cards belonging to the just-arrived king.
  4. Shuffle the deck; combat continues.
  After all four kings have been placed, empty deck reverts to the normal rule (flip discard pile face down, unshuffled).

## Behaviour cards (20 total; 8 visible faces transcribed from p12 vector art)

Format: range (left circle) / dodge difficulty (right circle) / bottom icons (movement cross value, damage value, extras). Only two faces per group are visible in the book; the remaining faces are not printed.

Standard (8 cards; 2 visible):
- **Horizontal Slash** — range 1, dodge 1, move 1, damage 5
- **Upward Slash** — range 1, dodge 1, move 1, damage 5

Second king (4 cards; 2 visible):
- **Evasive Slash** — range 1, dodge 1, move 1, damage 6, extra move 1 (after attack)
- **Thrust & Retreat** — range 2, dodge 2, move 1, damage 6, extra move 1 (retreat)

Third king (4 cards; 2 visible):
- **Precision Slash** — range 1, dodge 3, move 1, damage 5
- **Unerring Thrust** — range 2, dodge 3, move 1, damage 6

Fourth king (4 cards; 2 visible):
- **Shockwave** — range 1, dodge 1, damage 5 (no movement value visible)

Example mega behaviour card shown p11: **Homing Abyss Arrow** — range **∞**, dodge 1, move 0, magical node attack damage 5 (icon-level details from render; card belongs to the Four Kings deck).

## Treasure (pp5, 12)

- 2x Four Kings treasure cards. Visible: **Bequeathed Lord Soul** — equipment, stat line 20/20/20/20, text: "Tier 3 for each of your stats is 40 while you have this card equipped."
- Mega boss treasure is added to inventory on victory but only used in custom campaigns (p12).

## Campaign scenario — "Call of the Abyss" (Dark Souls 1, pp14-15)

Five-session extended campaign combining Four Kings + **Darkroot expansion** content. Encounters drawn from a shared core+Darkroot deck; L4 encounters from Four Kings.

- **Section 1 — Undead Parish**: Bonfire, L1, L1, L2, L2, **Titanite Demon (Mini Boss)**
- **Section 2 — Darkroot Basin**: Bonfire, L1, L2, L2, L3, **Sif, the Great Grey Wolf (Main Boss)**
- **Section 3 — Royal Wood**: Bonfire, L2, L3, L3, L3, **Knight Artorias (Main Boss)**
- **Section 4 — Penultimate Trial**: Bonfire, L3, L3, **L4 encounter**, **Ornstein & Smough (Main Boss)**
- **Section 5 — The Abyss**: Bonfire, L4, L4, **The Four Kings (Mega Boss)**

Special campaign placement (p14): in Section 4 the Mega Boss board (encounter side up) aligns its doorway with a **level 3 encounter's** doorway rather than the Bonfire tile; flip it after clearing the L4. L4 encounters never reset on bonfire rest.

## ENGINE NOTES

- New encounter tier `level4`: doubled spawn/terrain nodes, 4 entry nodes, one-shot (persist `completed` across bonfire rests), fixed reward `+8 souls x partySize`, cube-clear on win, drawn 1-of-4 with component-availability fallback.
- Mega Boss board = special two-sided tile: `encounterSide` / `bossSide(megaBossSpawnNode)`; doorway alignment to Bonfire (or L3 tile in campaigns).
- Post-main-boss phase: shop (Andre/Firekeeper) allowed, exploration disabled, rest costs spark (no free rest after L4 clear).
- Four Kings model: `kings[4]`, each `{health: 25, inPlay, dead}` sharing one behaviour deck; boss activation iterates in-play kings in index order applying the same card. Threat level not applicable — activation order by king index.
- Deck lifecycle state machine: `summonsRemaining: 3`; on deck-empty-at-activation: if summonsRemaining > 0 → spawn next king (leap-push occupants), remove 1 random card unseen, add 2 random cards from that king's 4-card pool, shuffle, decrement; else → recycle discard unshuffled.
- Zero-kings-in-play activation: flip card, heal every character +1, end activation ("Take a Breather").
- Win condition: all four kings dead (health dials 1–4 each at 0).
- Card pools must be tagged `standard | king2 | king3 | king4`; only 8 of 20 faces are printed in the rulebook — pull full deck data from the TTS mod assets/Lua.
- Range ∞ attack support (Homing Abyss Arrow) — attack that ignores range check.

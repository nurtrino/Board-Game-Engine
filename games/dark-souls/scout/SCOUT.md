## Port risks

1. No rulebook in the mod (zero PDFs) — rules must come from elsewhere.
2. Google Drive (632 refs) + Pastebin (44 refs) hosting — availability
   unverified; download-mod-assets.mjs only handles the Steam-host rewrite.
3. Nearly all cards unnamed — cell-crop + manual/vision identification pass
   needed to build a card golden.
4. Room tiles hidden in States chains (incl. one nested States object) —
   extractor must walk States to get the full tile library.
5. Dumb table: no Lua setup/rules data at all; all structure is spatial.
